AI-Powered Lake Restoration

Clean Lake Together is an innovative web application built to restore and protect the lakes of Coimbatore using the power of Artificial Intelligence. Our platform brings together technology, community, and environmental responsibility to tackle pollution and promote sustainable water management.

By leveraging AI, we enable smarter monitoring, faster issue detection, and data-driven decision-making to address lake pollution effectively. From identifying waste accumulation to supporting community-led clean-up initiatives, our platform transforms how we engage with and care for our water bodies.

Whether you're a citizen, volunteer, or organization, Clean Lake Together empowers you to contribute, collaborate, and create real impact.
